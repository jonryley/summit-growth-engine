# Summit Operating Controls

## Purpose

Summit Operating Controls turn the Gated Business Lifecycle into a practical commercial management system.

They ensure opportunities progress through evidence, not optimism.

---

## Core Controls

1. Quick Qualifier  
2. Gate Readiness Check  
3. Opportunity Classification  
4. Sales to Delivery Handover  
5. Growth on a Page  
6. Monthly Growth Review  

---

## 1. Quick Qualifier

Used in Stage 1 Qualify to test whether the opportunity is real, worth it, winnable and deliverable.

---

## 2. Gate Readiness Check

Used before each stage progression.

Decision options:

- Pass  
- Hold  
- Rework  
- Stop  

---

## 3. Opportunity Classification

Every opportunity should be classified as:

- Lite  
- Standard  
- Strategic  
- Complex  
- Transformational  

---

## 4. Sales to Delivery Handover

Used between Convert and Deliver to ensure scope, value, risk and ownership are transferred properly.

---

## 5. Growth on a Page

A one-page account growth view used in Amplify.

Includes:

- Delivered value  
- Stakeholder position  
- Expansion opportunities  
- Renewal path  
- Advocacy potential  

---

## 6. Monthly Growth Review

A regular review rhythm to inspect:

- Pipeline quality  
- Gate movement  
- Forecast confidence  
- Delivery readiness  
- Expansion opportunities  

---

## Core Principle

Controls create evidence.  
Evidence creates better decisions.  
Better decisions create growth.
