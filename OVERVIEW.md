# Summit Growth Engine  
## A Commercial Operating System for Predictable, Scalable Growth

Summit helps organisations move from activity-driven sales to evidence-led commercial execution.

It connects:

- People
- Process
- Controls
- CRM
- Data
- AI
- Governance
- Cadence
- Growth

---

## The Problem

Most organisations do not have a pipeline problem.

They have:

- Weak qualification
- Poor opportunity governance
- CRM noise
- Forecasts based on optimism
- Misalignment between sales and delivery
- Growth dependent on individual heroics

---

## The Summit Approach

Summit uses a Gated Business Lifecycle:

Signal → Qualify → Value → Convert → Deliver → Amplify

Each stage has:

- Defined purpose
- Required evidence
- Gate criteria
- Controls
- Review cadence

---

## Core Principle

Stages create structure.  
Gates create truth.  
Controls create evidence.  
Truth creates growth.
