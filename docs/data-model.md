# Summit Data Model

The CRM data model is structured around five levels:

## 1. Account
Organisation level information such as sector, strategy, installed base, account priority, and relationship position.

## 2. Contact
Stakeholder level information including role, influence, decision authority, and relationship strength.

## 3. Opportunity
Commercial opportunity information including stage, value, timing, probability, solution scope, and competitive position.

## 4. Activity
Interactions, meetings, calls, emails, notes, tasks, and next actions.

## 5. Stage Specific Evidence
Field sets linked to qualification, value shaping, conversion, delivery readiness, and growth.

## Design Rules

- Keep core account and contact data stable
- Keep opportunity data stage based
- Only require fields when they are needed
- Make gates evidence based
- Support both reporting and coaching
