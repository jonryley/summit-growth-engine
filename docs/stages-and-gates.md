# Summit Stages and Gates

## Stage 0 Signal
Objective: Identify a credible opportunity signal worth exploring.

Gate: Is there enough evidence to justify qualification?

## Stage 1 Qualify
Objective: Confirm whether the opportunity is real, worthwhile, winnable, and deliverable.

Gate: Should the business invest time and resources in shaping this opportunity?

## Stage 2 Value
Objective: Define the customer value case, solution direction, and commercial logic.

Gate: Is there a compelling and credible value case with customer relevance?

## Stage 3 Convert
Objective: Secure commitment and move to agreement.

Gate: Is there a clear path to close with manageable risk?

## Stage 4 Deliver
Objective: Ensure readiness for delivery and protect customer confidence.

Gate: Is the business ready to deliver successfully?

## Stage 5 Amplify
Objective: Expand value through retention, growth, referrals, and strategic account development.

Gate: Has the work created a platform for growth?
