# Summit Example  
## Applying the Gated Business Lifecycle

This example shows how opportunities move through Genesis Arc — from initial signal to scalable growth.

---

## Stage 0 — Signal

**Objective**  
Identify real opportunities early  

**Activities**
- Client contact or inbound signal  
- Market or account insight  
- Early engagement with stakeholders  

**Output**
- Opportunity identified  
- Initial access established  

---

## Stage 1 — Qualify

**Objective**  
Focus only on opportunities that can be won  

**Key Questions**
- Is it real?  
- Is it worth it?  
- Can we win it?  
- Can we deliver it?  

**Activities**
- Quick qualification  
- CRM record created  
- Stakeholder mapping  

**Gate Criteria**
- Clear problem defined  
- Buyer identified  
- Commercial viability confirmed  

**Output**
- Qualified opportunity  
- Approved to progress  

---

## Stage 2 — Value

**Objective**  
Define and shape the commercial outcome  

**Activities**
- Develop value proposition  
- Build cost and delivery model  
- Assess risks and dependencies  
- Create commercial roadmap  

**Gate Criteria**
- Value case clearly defined  
- Solution aligned to need  
- Resources approved  

**Output**
- Developed opportunity  
- Go / No-Go decision confirmed  

---

## Stage 3 — Convert

**Objective**  
Convert opportunity into contracted revenue  

**Activities**
- Proposal or tender submission  
- Commercial negotiation  
- Legal and contractual alignment  
- Closing plan execution  

**Gate Criteria**
- Offer accepted  
- Commercial agreement reached  
- Contract signed  

**Output**
- Closed Won or Closed Lost  
- Transition to delivery  

---

## Stage 4 — Deliver

**Objective**  
Deliver value and expand the relationship  

**Activities**
- Mobilisation and onboarding  
- Delivery execution  
- KPI and performance tracking  
- Identify expansion opportunities  

**Gate Criteria**
- Value delivered against expectations  
- Customer engagement maintained  
- Expansion opportunities identified  

**Output**
- Active delivery  
- Growth pipeline created  

---

## Stage 5 — Amplify

**Objective**  
Turn delivery into scalable growth  

**Activities**
- Capture customer satisfaction  
- Develop case studies  
- Enable referrals and advocacy  
- Expand services and accounts  

**Gate Criteria**
- Customer is referenceable  
- Measurable value achieved  
- Repeat or expansion business secured  

**Output**
- Advocacy established  
- Repeatable growth enabled  

---

## The Principle

If an opportunity does not meet the gate criteria  
it does not progress  

---

## Summary Flow

Signal → Qualify → Value → Convert → Deliver → Amplify  

Each stage introduces clarity  
Each gate enforces discipline  
Together they create scalable growth
