# Gate 4 Delivery Readiness Gate

## Purpose

Decide whether the business is ready to mobilise successfully.

## Pass Criteria

- Scope is understood
- Delivery owner is assigned
- Handover is complete
- Risks and dependencies are logged
- Success measures are defined
- Customer governance is in place

## Decision Options

- Progress to Amplify
- Hold
- Rework
