# Gate 5 Growth Gate

## Purpose

Decide whether the delivered work has created a platform for growth.

## Pass Criteria

- Customer value has been evidenced
- Relationship position has improved
- Follow on opportunities are identified
- Renewal or expansion path exists
- Reference potential has been assessed

## Decision Options

- Expand
- Sustain
- Monitor
- Exit
