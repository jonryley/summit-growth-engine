# Summit Operating Model  
## The Operating Stack for Predictable, Scalable Growth

Summit is not just a sales process.

It is a commercial operating model that connects people, process, CRM, data, AI and governance into one disciplined growth system.

---

# The Summit Operating Stack

```text
People
↓
Process
↓
CRM
↓
Data
↓
AI
↓
Governance
↓
Growth
```

---

## 1. People

Growth starts with people.

Summit aligns leadership, sales, business development, delivery and account teams around one shared commercial language.

### Focus

- Clear ownership  
- Strong qualification discipline  
- Better stakeholder alignment  
- Reduced individual dependency  
- Shared accountability for growth  

---

## 2. Process

Process creates repeatability.

Summit uses the Gated Business Lifecycle to move opportunities from signal to scalable growth.

### Lifecycle

```text
Stage 0 Signal
Stage 1 Qualify
Stage 2 Value
Stage 3 Convert
Stage 4 Deliver
Stage 5 Amplify
```

### Core Principle

Stages create structure.  
Gates create truth.  
Truth creates growth.

---

## 3. CRM

CRM should not just record activity.

It should support commercial execution.

Summit turns CRM into a system of progression by aligning:

- Stages  
- Gates  
- Required fields  
- Opportunity quality  
- Forecast confidence  
- Leadership rhythm  

### Outcome

CRM becomes a system of execution, not just a system of record.

---

## 4. Data

Good decisions need good data.

Summit defines the minimum data required at each stage so teams can make better decisions without overloading CRM.

### Data Focus

- Customer problem  
- Buyer access  
- Opportunity value  
- Stage health  
- Decision path  
- Close date  
- Delivery confidence  
- Expansion potential  

### Principle

Capture the right data at the right time.

---

## 5. AI

AI only works when the commercial process underneath it is clear.

Summit creates the structure needed for AI enabled growth by improving the quality, consistency and usefulness of commercial data.

### AI Use Cases

- Opportunity qualification support  
- Deal coaching  
- Forecast risk detection  
- Next best action prompts  
- Pipeline quality scoring  
- Account growth insight  

### Principle

AI amplifies discipline.  
It does not replace it.

---

## 6. Governance

Governance keeps the system honest.

Summit uses gates, reviews and leadership cadence to ensure opportunities progress based on evidence, not optimism.

### Governance Mechanisms

- Stage reviews  
- Gate criteria  
- Pipeline inspection  
- Forecast review  
- Risk escalation  
- Delivery readiness checks  
- Account growth reviews  

### Principle

If it does not pass the gate, it does not move.

---

## 7. Growth

The purpose of Summit is scalable growth.

Not just more activity.  
Not just more CRM data.  
Not just more pipeline noise.

Summit focuses on:

- Better qualification  
- Higher win rates  
- Improved forecast confidence  
- Stronger delivery alignment  
- Increased customer lifetime value  
- Repeatable commercial growth  

---

## How The Model Works

```text
People create alignment
Process creates structure
CRM creates visibility
Data creates insight
AI creates leverage
Governance creates truth
Truth creates growth
```

---

## Why This Matters

Most organisations do not fail because they lack opportunities.

They fail because:

- Weak opportunities progress too far  
- CRM becomes administration  
- Forecasts are based on hope  
- Sales and delivery are disconnected  
- Leadership reviews activity instead of evidence  
- Growth depends on individual heroics  

Summit fixes this by creating a connected commercial operating model.
